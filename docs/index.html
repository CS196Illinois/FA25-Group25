About us Forexplorer was started by four students at UIUC in 2025 as a project. We aim to make travel easier to plan and more accessible for the average person by leveraging the infinite capabilities of machine learning. Our tool is trained on a decade of foreign exchange data using Python (Pandas, Numpy).
